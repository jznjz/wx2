XPOWER DESK SYSTEM Modular Power Track

Role: Industrial Design

The Desk System is an addition to the XPOWER product line-up with an emphasis to branch the company’s business model into B2C, as the other pre-existing products all required on-site installation. The aluminium stands allows the shorter track to be used on desks in combination with other modules such as speakers, lights, chargers etc.
