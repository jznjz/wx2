XPOWER DESK SYSTEM

Modular Power Track

Role: Industrial Design

The Desk System is an addition to the XPOWER product line-up with an emphasis to branch the company’s business model into B2C, as the other pre-existing products all required on-site installation. The aluminium stands allows the shorter track to be used on desks in combination with other modules such as speakers, lights, chargers etc. The XPOWER Smart Power System is a popular household electronic product. It includes power tracks for integrated power lines, smart switches, smart sockets, USB, smart lights and much more to form a truly connected smart home. The track has been developed and designed in-house with the option to be embedded in desks, cabinets, and countertops. This solves the common issue of messy cables and insufficient power sockets in our daily environments. Various lengths can be mass produced to extend and fit your entire home. The modular power track perfectly combines the aesthetics of a standard baseboard with functionality and durability. The cumbersome process of routing electrical wiring and the need to compromise an ideal interior layout for socket placement are a thing of the past with the flexibility of modular sockets that can be inserted anywhere into the track where access to power is needed. The infrastructure is already in place to integrate smart cameras and smart curtains, etc. in the near future to realize the foundation of a smart home power system.
