Smart Energy Multi-level Energy Management App

Role: UI Design / Mock-up

The Energy Management System is based on the XPower product line from Shanutec Technology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd., co-developed with Siemens for staff energy management purposes. The in-depth enterprise-level management, from single employees to whole departments provides an overall energy overview for convenient control. Other features include controllable power access, reliable power metering and configurable power-saving schedules.

  • Splash Screen Animation was created in After Effects and exported as a .json file to be used on the iOS and Android App with much better efficiency (0.2 MB) and scalability than a gif or mp4.

  • The high-fidelity prototype was coded using FramerJS to convey the core design components to the developers as well as the stakeholders in the most interactive way possible.
