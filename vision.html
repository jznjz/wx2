XPOWER VISION

Modular Power Track

Role: Project Lead

VISION is an award-winning (iF 2018 Gold) collaborative project with Jacob Jensen Design to reshape the XPOWER modular power track based on the existing patented technology. Designed to meet the growing demand for accessible outlets, it provides power wherever it is needed. The track can be either mounted as wall skirting, clamped to the end of a desk, or run parallel beneath a conference table. It matches the design trend of turning offices into open interactive spaces. The unviersal socket clicks onto the track, allowing the user to access electricity at any location, thereby reducing cable clutter. Up until now, electricity used inside buildings and at home is accessed through electrical installation of cabling and associated devices such as switches, distribution boards and sockets directly in the wall structure. This conventional way of wiring makes the number and position of wall sockets fixed. In real-life usage, if the amount of electrical appliances exceeds the available sockets, users are forced to connect additional extension cords, exposing a messy clutter of wires and more importantly potential safety hazards. The standard socket is often in a charged state, and an electric arc can be generated during the plugging or unplugging process, which may lead to various dangerous outcomes such as short circuits, electric shocks, fire, etc., hence greatly increasing the risk factor. A lot of equipment such as computers, televisions and water heaters are also constantly plugged in, and hence never completely powered off, consuming power in a standby state, which adds up for a significant amount of the electricity bill. This lack of environmental consideration continues to have a negative effect on global carbon emissions. Furthermore, the traditional socket cannot be remotely controlled, and must be turned on and off in person, which does not reflect the recent trend of user-centric design. The traditional socket has undergone continuous evolution over the past few decades, yet other than structural optimizations to reduce the size of the socket for ergonomic reasons, there has not been much breakthrough in the underlying technology itself. The XPOWER smart power system completely revolutionizes our traditional way of how we access power, combining the characteristics of IoT not only provides great convenience for people's lives, but also effectively avoids any unnecessary energy consumption of household appliances. It is an inevitable next step of technological innovation for the traditional electronics industry. The implementation of this project will have a profound impact on the planning of all smart living spaces, the design of modern interior architecture and the manufacture of furniture systems.
