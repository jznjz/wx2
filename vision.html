XPOWER VISION Modular Power Track

Role: Project Lead

VISION is an award-winning (iF 2018 Gold) collaborative project with Jacob Jensen Design to reshape the XPOWER modular power track based on the existing patented technology. Designed to meet the growing demand for accessible outlets, it provides power wherever it is needed. The track can be either mounted as wall skirting, clamped to the end of a desk, or run parallel beneath a conference table. It matches the design trend of turning offices into open interactive spaces. The unviersal socket clicks onto the track, allowing the user to access electricity at any location, thereby reducing cable clutter.
