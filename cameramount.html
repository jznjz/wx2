DRIFT Hand Mount Ergonomic Camera Mount

Role: Industrial Design / Prototyping

Ergonomic mounting solution to attach the DRIFT Action cameras securely to the hand during different activities such as surfing, skydiving etc. The initial requirement to mount the cameras onto the wrist similar to competitors quickly changed during the early design stage as the optimal viewing angle would be obstructed. Multiple prototypes and variations were printed for testing purposes to determine the most optimal fit.
