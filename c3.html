C3 Compact Cooling Case

Role: Concept / Simulation / Prototyping

C3 is a small form factor computer case for increased cooling efficiency in high-performance desktop PCs. Through compartmentalizing active and passive components, the internal layout provides a structured channel for the airflow to reach every component in the most efficient manner. By implementing principles of fluid dynamics that are used in modern F1 cars, a pressure differential induces a secondary airflow, that aids the cooling process of other core heat dissipating components such as the Graphics card.
